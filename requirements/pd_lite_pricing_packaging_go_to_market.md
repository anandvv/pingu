## Purpose

This document defines how PD Lite is packaged, priced, and taken to market as an independent startup. The goal is to align pricing with customer value, enable frictionless adoption, and support efficient growth from early-stage teams to large enterprises.

Pricing is a growth lever, not a billing artifact.

---

## Pricing Philosophy

1. Customers pay for outcomes, not seats
2. Pricing scales with real usage and value delivered
3. Entry must be easy; expansion must be natural
4. Costs must remain predictable
5. AI usage should feel like leverage, not tax

---

## Core Value Units

PD Lite pricing is based on four primary consumption units:

### 1. Incidents Managed

- An incident counts when declared and closed
- Includes full lifecycle management
- Aligns directly with customer value

---

### 2. Notifications Delivered

- Voice, SMS, RCS, WhatsApp, push, and email
- Billed on successful delivery attempts
- Provider redundancy included

---

### 3. AI Actions Executed

- AI-generated summaries
- Classification and recommendations
- Post-incident review drafts

AI actions are metered but bundled generously to encourage adoption.

---

### 4. Integrations Activated

- Monitoring and observability tools
- Ticketing and workflow systems
- Included up to a threshold per plan

---

## Packaging Tiers

### Free (Learning Tier)

**Target:** Early teams and evaluation

Includes:
- Limited incidents per month
- Limited notifications
- Core chat-based incident management
- AI summaries and suggestions

Purpose:
- Learn the product
- Run real incidents safely

---

### Team (SMB)

**Target:** Small to mid-size engineering teams

Includes:
- Higher incident and notification limits
- Full AI copilot during incidents
- Standard integrations
- Email and chat support

Pricing characteristics:
- Monthly subscription + usage overages

---

### Business

**Target:** Scaling companies

Includes:
- Higher usage caps
- Advanced AI agents
- Audit logs
- Priority support
- SSO

---

### Enterprise

**Target:** Large, regulated organizations

Includes:
- Custom usage limits
- Advanced governance and compliance
- Dedicated support
- Custom retention and residency

Pricing characteristics:
- Annual contract
- Predictable usage bands

## Usage Controls and Predictability

- Real-time usage dashboards
- Alerts when nearing limits

- No surprise overages by default

## Go-To-Market Strategy

### Initial Beachhead

- Teams currently using Slack-native incident tools
- Companies frustrated with per-seat pricing
- Engineering-led organizations

---

### Acquisition Channels

- Developer-led word of mouth
- Content and incident best-practices
- Integration marketplaces (Slack, monitoring tools)

---

### Sales Motion

- Product-led growth for Free and Team tiers
- Light-touch sales for Business
- Enterprise sales only when necessary

---

## Expansion Strategy

Expansion is driven by:
- Increased incident volume
- Broader notification usage
- Adoption of AI agents
- Organization-wide rollout

No forced seat expansion.

---
